Social Content Engine -- Pipeline Flow

Bi-weekly cron (every other Sunday AM) or manual /social-content
|
ORCHESTRATOR
.claude/skills/social-content/SKILL.md
- Determines date range (next 2 weeks, ~7 posts)
- Rotates across 6 pillars: Proof of Work, AI Education, Website Tips, Local Relevance, Behind the Scenes, + 1 engagement
- Generates copy per post, adapts per platform (FB, IG, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Assigns image pipeline per post type
- Outputs batch JSON for downstream modules
