## Website Tiers

| Tier | Price | Pages | Delivery |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $2,200-$2,500 | up to 5 | 1-2 weeks |
| Professional | $3,500-$4,000 | up to 10 | 2-3 weeks |
| E-Commerce | $4,500-$6,000 | + catalog | 3+ weeks |

- **Starter:** Basic SEO, 2 revision rounds
- **Professional:** Full SEO, AI-assisted copywriting, unlimited revisions, GBP setup
- **E-Commerce:** Everything in Professional + product catalog, cart, Stripe integration
