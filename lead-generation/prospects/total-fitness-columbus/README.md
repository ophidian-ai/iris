## Project Scope Summary

**Core Deliverable:** Modern, fast, mobile-first website redesign that converts visitors into members and showcases the facility's impressive amenities.

**Pages:**
- Homepage (hero with facility photos, services overview, class schedule preview, testimonials, CTA)
- About / Our Story (27-year history, Mark Perry's background)
- Our Facility (photo gallery, amenity breakdown, virtual tour potential)
- Classes (full schedule with filtering, class descriptions)
- Personal Training (trainer profiles, session info, inquiry form)
- Nutrition Counseling (program details, consultation request form)
- Childcare (hours, ages, policies, registration)
- Membership (tier comparison table, inquiry/sign-up form)
- Contact (form, map, hours, directions)

**Technical:**
- Responsive, mobile-first design
- Fast load times (replace Avada/Slider Revolution bloat)
- Google Analytics 4 setup
- SEO fundamentals (meta tags, schema, sitemap, local SEO)
- Google Business Profile optimization guidance
- Social media links in header/footer
- Contact form with email routing

**Estimated Tier:** Professional ($3,500-$4,000) -- 9 pages, modern design, full SEO, forms

## Upsell Opportunities

### Tier 1 -- Quick Wins

- **Online class booking system** -- Let members reserve spots in classes, reducing overcrowding and improving experience. Integration with a scheduling platform (Mindbody, Zen Planner, or similar).
- **Google Analytics 4 setup** -- Currently zero analytics. Install GA4 with conversion tracking for membership inquiries.
- **Google Business Profile optimization** -- With 361 Google reviews, this is a strong asset. Optimize categories, photos, posts, and Q&A.
- **Social media integration** -- Link Facebook (209 reviews), Instagram, and other profiles. Add social proof to the site.

### Tier 2 -- Growth Add-Ons

- **Member portal / login** -- Online account management: freeze membership, update billing, view class schedule, track attendance.
- **Online membership sign-up** -- Allow prospects to sign up and pay online (Stripe integration). Removes friction from the sales process.
- **Email marketing setup** -- Newsletter signup with automated welcome series, class announcements, and promotions. Capture leads who visit but don't call.
- **Review display integration** -- Pull in Google and Facebook reviews to the website. 571 reviews is a massive trust signal being wasted.
- **Virtual facility tour** -- 360-degree or video tour for prospects who want to see the gym before visiting.

### Tier 3 -- Premium Integrations

- **AI-powered chatbot** -- Answer common questions (hours, pricing, classes, childcare) 24/7. Capture leads after hours.
- **Gym management software integration** -- Connect website with gym management platform for unified member data, billing, and scheduling.
- **Automated SMS/email class reminders** -- Reduce no-shows for classes and personal training sessions.
- **Content marketing system** -- Blog with fitness tips, nutrition advice, member spotlights, and local SEO content.
- **Mobile app** -- Branded app for class booking, check-in, and member engagement (longer-term).

---

## Sales Angle

Lead with the missing fundamentals: no analytics, no contact form, no online booking, no social links, and a broken shop page. For a 27-year-old business with 571 reviews and a massive facility (pool, waterpark, basketball court, 10k+ sq ft weight room), the website dramatically undersells what they offer. The Avada theme is functional but bloated and generic -- it does not reflect the quality of the facility. The biggest revenue opportunity is online class booking and membership sign-up, which eliminates the "call for pricing" barrier that loses prospects who expect a modern digital experience. The dev URL leaks and content inconsistencies (365 vs 363 days) signal a site that was built hastily and never polished.
