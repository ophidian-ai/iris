## Upsell Opportunities

### Tier 1 -- Quick Wins
- **Online gift card e-commerce** -- Stripe or Square integration for direct gift card purchases (removes dependency on broken Vagaro widget)
- **Google Analytics 4 migration** -- Replace deprecated UA tracking with GA4 + basic conversion tracking
- **Google Business Profile optimization** -- Improve local search visibility with proper categories, photos, and review responses

### Tier 2 -- Growth Add-Ons
- **Email marketing integration** -- Mailchimp or Klaviyo setup with automated welcome series, birthday promotions, and appointment reminders
- **Digital intake forms** -- Online health/consent forms that clients complete before their appointment (saves front desk time)
- **Automated appointment reminders** -- SMS/email reminders to reduce no-shows (via Vagaro, Twilio, or booking platform)
- **Review generation system** -- Automated post-visit review request emails/texts to boost Google and Facebook reviews

### Tier 3 -- Premium Integrations
- **AI-powered FAQ chatbot** -- Website chatbot that answers common questions (hours, services, pricing, booking) 24/7
- **Online scheduling optimization** -- Full booking system audit and optimization (or migration from Vagaro to a better platform)
- **Social media content system** -- Monthly social post templates and scheduling for Facebook/Instagram
- **Loyalty program** -- Digital punch card or points system to increase repeat visits
