Our Story

More Than Just a Meal

Homeboyz Fried Chicken & Fish is Columbus, Indiana's destination for authentic soul food. Owned by Theresa Creecy, this is more than a restaurant -- it's a community gathering place where comfort food brings people together.

Every piece of chicken is seasoned and fried to crispy perfection. Every side dish is made from scratch. Featured on the Visit Columbus tourism site, Homeboyz has quickly become a local favorite for both residents and visitors alike.
