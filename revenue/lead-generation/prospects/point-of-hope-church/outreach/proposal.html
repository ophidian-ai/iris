What We Found

Your current website at pointofhopechurch.com has served its purpose, but there are several issues holding it back:

  • Not mobile-responsive -- The site does not adapt to phones or tablets. Over 60% of web traffic now comes from mobile devices.
  • Broken functionality -- JavaScript errors (missing DD_roundies library) and mixed content warnings are degrading the user experience.
  • Outdated design -- The layout uses image-based components from an older era of web design, making it difficult to update content.
  • Dead links -- Several "Read More" buttons link to nowhere (#).
  • No Facebook integration -- Despite active social media presence, there is no connection between your Facebook page and website.
  • No pastor or leadership section -- Visitors cannot easily learn about who leads the church.
  • Limited visitor experience -- Key information (service times, what to expect, how to find you) is scattered across multiple pages.

What We'll Build

A modern, single-page website that consolidates all essential information into one clean, scrollable experience:

  • Hero Section -- Eye-catching introduction with your church name, tagline, service times, and clear calls to action.
  • Welcome / About -- Your mission, vision, and core beliefs presented in a compelling, readable format.
  • Service Schedule -- All service times (Sunday School, Worship, Bible Study, Punto de Esperanza) in an easy-to-scan layout.
  • Facebook Integration -- Live connection to your Facebook page for events, announcements, and community updates.
  • Meet the Pastor -- Dedicated section for Rev. Stephen Gossage with bio, photo, and contact information.
  • What to Expect -- Visitor-focused section covering worship style, dress code, kids programs, and discipleship.
  • Ministries -- Overview of all church ministries including youth, Sunday School, worship, and outreach.
  • Punto de Esperanza -- Bilingual section for your Spanish-language ministry with separate contact info for Rev. Calvillo.
  • Location & Contact -- Interactive Google Maps embed, click-to-call phone, email link, and full address.
