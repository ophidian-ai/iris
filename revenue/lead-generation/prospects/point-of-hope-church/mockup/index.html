Welcome

Discover Your Personal
Hope at the Cross

If you haven't yet chosen a church home, we invite you to our thriving, growing multi-cultural congregation. Experience dynamic worship centering on a loving Savior interested in humanity's well-being.

When you visit, you will experience anointed teaching and preaching, vibrant worship and praise, and practical ministries focused toward the family. Point of Hope is a place for you.

We are a community church with a world vision whose purpose is to stand for Biblical truth -- expanding our boundaries to reach beyond traditional Pentecostalism with a message of love, mercy, and forgiveness.

"Repent, and be baptized every one of you in the name of Jesus Christ for the remission of sins, and ye shall receive the gift of the Holy Ghost." Acts 2:38

Leadership

Meet Our Pastor

👤

Rev. Stephen Gossage

Senior Pastor

Pastor Gossage leads Point of Hope Apostolic Church with a vision to be a 21st Century Apostolic Church -- one founded upon the belief that the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God.

Under his leadership, Point of Hope has grown into a thriving, multi-cultural congregation with a heart for the Great Commission. His passion is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with love, mercy, and compassion.

Point of Hope stands for Biblical truth while expanding boundaries to reach beyond traditional Pentecostalism -- with an inclusive gospel message: whosoever will, let him come.

Your First Visit

What to Expect

Visiting a new place for the first time can be intimidating. Here's what you can look forward to.

🙌

Warm Welcome

The most friendly church you've ever attended. People from all walks of life and faith who love their church. You'll meet smiling faces and find a community to live out your faith in real and relevant ways.

🎶

Passionate Worship

The most passionate worship you've ever experienced. A living, moving, passionate God deserves vibrant and real response. Great music and the opportunity to participate as much as you like.

📖

Relevant Teaching

The most fresh and creative presentation of the message of Jesus. True to God's Word, but relevant to your life. If you feel lost, tired of running, or need a change of pace -- meet your new direction.

👗

Come As You Are

Some dress their "Sunday best," others wear casual weekend attire. Feel free to wear whatever you feel most comfortable worshipping in. No judgment, just community.

👶

Kids Programs

A thriving Sunday School "Super Church" program and exciting youth ministry. Your kids will have tons of fun while learning spiritual principles in age-appropriate settings.

📚

Discipleship

Four levels of discipleship classes: Encounter (Doctrine), Equip (Commitment), Engage (Ministry), and Empower (Leadership). A clear path for spiritual growth.

Punto de Esperanza

Bienvenido

Somos la Iglesia en espanol hermana de Point Of Hope Apostolic Church. Nuestra doctrina consiste con la Iglesia de ingles. Nosotros creemos en un solo Dios, Jesus en el unico nombre que bautizamos y en el unico nombre que puedes ser lleno del Espiritu Santo.

Nos gustaria conocerte en uno de nuestros servicios. Son bienvenidos a tener una experiencia en un servicio Pentecostal.

Welcome

We are the Spanish daughter work of Point of Hope Apostolic Church. We are an Apostolic church in principle, practice, and power. Our doctrinal belief is consistent with our English-speaking church.

We believe in One God, Jesus' Name baptism, and Holy Ghost infilling. We would love to meet you in our services.
