About Carlos Pizza

Columbus's Best Kept Secret

Carlos Pizza is the kind of place locals keep to themselves. Tucked away on 25th Street, we've been quietly serving some of the best pizza in Columbus, Indiana, building a loyal following one slice at a time.

Everything we make is prepared fresh daily with quality ingredients and real care. No shortcuts, no freezers, no compromises. Just honest, delicious food at fair prices.
