Over 40 Years of Quality You Can Count On

Since 1980, Jeff and Gloria Voelz have been restoring vehicles and serving the Columbus, Indiana community with honest, high-quality auto body repair. What started as a family business remains one today -- built on trust, craftsmanship, and doing right by every customer.

Our I-CAR Gold Class certification means our technicians meet the highest training standards in the collision repair industry. Combined with BBB accreditation, you can be confident your vehicle is in expert hands.

We work with all insurance companies and will guide you through the claims process from start to finish. Our goal is simple: get you back on the road safely.
