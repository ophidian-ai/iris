Our Story

A Decade of Dedicated
Wellness & Beauty

For over eleven years, Columbus Massage Center & Salon has been Columbus, Indiana's trusted destination for relaxation and rejuvenation. What began as a passion for healing through touch has grown into a full-service wellness and beauty sanctuary.

Every treatment is delivered by skilled, licensed professionals who genuinely care about your well-being. We believe everyone deserves a space to unwind, recharge, and feel their best -- and we have built exactly that.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you will feel the difference. This is your time. Your sanctuary.

-- Dana Greathouse, Owner
