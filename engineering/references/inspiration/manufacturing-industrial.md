## Daisheng Enterprise

- **URL:** <https://daisheng.com.tw/>
- **What it is:** Taiwan-based wood furniture manufacturer with a factory in Vietnam. OEM/ODM services and finished products targeting international wholesale/retail partners.
- **Style tags:** clean, minimal, editorial, industrial-meets-craft, nature-forward, premium-B2B, bilingual
- **Notable elements:**
  - Full-screen hero carousel with large typographic overlay (English headline, Japanese subhead)
  - Parenthetical section labels as a design motif — `( About )`, `( Business )`, `( Factory )` — clean and distinctive
  - Marquee/scrolling "Contact Us" banner at the footer
  - Alternating image/text layout for product/services sections
  - Forest and natural wood imagery as a brand anchor throughout
  - Dual-language navigation (JP default, EN toggle)
  - ISO certification callout for quality-tier signaling
- **Best for:** Furniture makers, woodworkers, craftspeople, any manufacturer or trade business that wants to elevate beyond a plain catalogue site — especially when natural materials are core to the brand
