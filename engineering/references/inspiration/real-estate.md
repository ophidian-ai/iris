## Volta SKAI
- **URL:** https://voltaskai.endover.ee/en/
- **What it is:** Premium double high-rise residential development in Tallinn, Estonia (Volta Quarter). Seaside apartments and penthouse suites with panoramic sea views. Developer: Endover. Move-in mid-2027.
- **Style tags:** modern-architectural, aspirational, glass-steel-minimalism, premium-residential, light-filled
- **Notable elements:**
  - Scroll-triggered section transitions with "Scroll for more" prompt — guides the user through the story
  - Interactive floor selector: choose floor level to see the view from that height
  - Construction timeline as visual milestone steps (not a boring list)
  - Numbered exterior image carousel
  - Stat counters: 2 buildings, 12 floors, 2027 move-in
  - Smart home + keyless entry feature callouts integrated into the layout
  - Dual language (EST/ENG) toggle
- **Best for:** New construction developments, luxury condos, architecture firms, any real estate project with a story to tell (not just a listing)

## Saisei

- **URL:** <https://saisei-sbj.webflow.io/>
- **What it is:** Tokyo-based architecture firm concept centered on sustainable and regenerative design — blending Japanese tradition with modern innovation to reshape urban environments. (Portfolio/creative exploration piece by Studio Bamo.J, Awwwards nominated.)
- **Style tags:** minimalist, editorial, Japanese-aesthetic, wabi-sabi, high-contrast, typographic-led, scroll-driven
- **Notable elements:**
  - Kanji characters as section markers (伝統, 革新, 建築) alongside English — bilingual as design element
  - Scroll-scrub image gallery with multiple full-bleed architectural project images
  - Fragment + Aeonik typeface pairing (display serif + geometric sans)
  - Numbered project carousel (1/3, 2/3, 3/3)
  - Full-screen hero: single large architectural interior + centered wordmark
  - Philosophy quotes using Japanese concepts (Ichi-go ichi-e, etc.)
  - Awwwards nominated
- **Best for:** Architecture firms, interior design studios, high-end construction, any brand wanting a contemplative Japanese-minimalist aesthetic
