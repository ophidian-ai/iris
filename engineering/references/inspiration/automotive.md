# Automotive Inspiration

Sites from auto detailing, car care, and automotive brands. Reference when building sites for auto shops, detailers, and car-related businesses.

## The Detail Dr.

- **URL:** <https://thedetaildr.net/>
- **What it is:** Australian car care e-commerce brand founded by Dr. Paul Bruno (professional detailer with a science background). Products: ceramic coatings, wash/prep, trim/tyre/interior care, accessories.
- **Style tags:** clinical, technical, precise, clean, science-forward, e-commerce
- **Notable elements:**
  - Hero headline: "Where passion meets science" — bridges enthusiasm and credibility
  - Category-driven navigation as primary UX (product categories front and center)
  - Dual CTAs: shop now + how-to guides (serves both buyers and researchers)
  - SVG logo with tagline variant
  - Built by Milkable agency
  - Clean white/neutral palette with product photography
- **Best for:** Auto detailing shops, specialty e-commerce, any trade brand wanting a technical/professional feel without being cold
