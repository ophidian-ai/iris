# Design Agency Inspiration

Sites from design studios and agencies. Reference when building any creative/portfolio-forward project or when seeking high execution quality benchmarks.

---

## SIRNIK

- **URL:** <https://www.sirnik.co/>
- **What it is:** Webflow design & development studio based in Poland/Ukraine. Services: UI/UX, Webflow dev, full-scope digital products.
- **Style tags:** minimalist, typographic, structured, editorial, character-animation, monochrome
- **Notable elements:**
  - Hero: "Design as balance between structure and emotion"
  - Philosophy: "Observe. Distil. Design." — each word on its own line, full-screen
  - Each nav link letter is an individual DOM element enabling character-level stagger animations
  - Numbered service entries (01, 02, 03) with accordion-style expand
  - CSS Design Awards featured badge
  - Work section cycles through client names as animated headings
  - Webflow-built
- **Best for:** Any professional services site, design-forward B2B, agency pitch pages

## Glow

- **URL:** <https://ai.glow.team/>
- **What it is:** Design agency specializing in product design, brand, web, and pitch decks for AI startups. Clients have raised $600M+. Starts at $12k/month.
- **Style tags:** bold, kinetic, startup-native, energetic, editorial-typography, dark-light-contrast
- **Notable elements:**
  - Background video hero
  - Letter-by-letter stagger animation on nav links
  - Sound toggle (on/off ambient audio)
  - Social proof stat counter ("k users in 2 days")
  - Founder signature + avatar for personal brand trust
  - Multilingual social proof rotating through country flags (EN/IT/ES)
  - Calendly CTA for free strategy call
  - Webflow-built
- **Best for:** AI startup landing pages, high-energy SaaS, founder-brand sites

## ForAI (DesignForAI)

- **URL:** <https://forai.design/>
- **What it is:** Boutique creative studio led by designer Doruk, building brand identity, landing pages, and web experiences exclusively for AI companies. Clients include Cisco, IO.NET, AG2/AutoGen, Layer AI, Frigade, Framer.
- **Style tags:** minimalist, precision-focused, dark, editorial, motion-forward, portfolio-led
- **Notable elements:**
  - Portfolio-led homepage — selected works image-heavy, front and center
  - Rotating testimonial carousel with named clients
  - Horizontal scrolling client logo strip
  - Manifesto copy section: "No shortcuts. No soulless, throwaway fluff."
  - Single CTA: "Start a project" — no forms, no lead capture on homepage
  - Minimal nav: Works, Testimonials, Pricing, Mission, Services, Archive, Contact
  - Framer-built
- **Best for:** AI/tech agency positioning, portfolio sites, any founder who wants the work to speak louder than the sales copy

## Aidentity

- **URL:** <https://aidentity.sg/>
- **What it is:** Singapore-based web design and development agency (est. 2011). Builds corporate and ecommerce sites across WordPress, custom PHP/Laravel. Clients include Singapore Airlines, Singtel, NUS, Samsung.
- **Style tags:** corporate, clean, service-forward, portfolio-driven, traditional, trust-heavy
- **Notable elements:**
  - Stats bar prominently placed: 210+ clients, 350+ websites, 28 staff, 10+ years
  - Portfolio grid with recognizable enterprise client logos as the primary trust signal
  - WhatsApp chat widget — standard conversion tool for SE Asian markets
  - Split service sections: Website Design vs. Web Development presented as parallel tracks
  - Standard agency structure: hero → about → portfolio → services → CTA
  - **Reference value:** Represents the conventional agency template — useful as a "what not to do" benchmark when we want to differentiate OphidianAI's own positioning
- **Best for:** Understanding the baseline corporate agency template; reference when a client needs a safe, enterprise-credibility-first layout with minimal design risk
