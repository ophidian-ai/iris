# Professional Services Inspiration

Sites from finance, consulting, law, and other B2B professional service firms. Reference when building sites for service businesses that need to project credibility and expertise.

---

## Fairvue Partners

- **URL:** <https://www.fairvuepartners.com/>
- **What it is:** Strategic investor relations consultancy for public companies — designs and executes IR programs to raise market visibility, optimize valuation, and improve capital markets communication.
- **Style tags:** premium, institutional, minimal, high-end-corporate, dark-neutral, editorial-typography
- **Notable elements:**
  - Animated headline text with exaggerated letter-spacing — characters spaced far apart as a visual effect on load/scroll
  - Vertical text orientation for CTAs and section labels (rotated/stacked characters)
  - Hero slideshow with professional photography (corporate/real estate settings)
  - Animated stats counter section
  - Clean single-column nav with "Contact Us" CTA isolated on the right
  - Case study cards with category labels (Transaction, Story, Insight)
  - Minimal footer — LinkedIn only, no social overload
- **Best for:** Finance, consulting, law firms, investor relations, any B2B professional services brand that needs to signal credibility and restraint over personality
