# Health & Wellness Inspiration

Sites from medical practices, wellness brands, spas, and health-focused businesses. Reference when building sites for doctors, therapists, med spas, fitness studios, or any health-adjacent client.

## Dr. Myra Reed

- **URL:** <https://drmyrareed.com/>
- **What it is:** Functional and concierge medicine practice in Panama City Beach, FL. Membership-based model covering root-cause treatment, hormone optimization, medical weight loss, aesthetics, and IV therapy.
- **Style tags:** clean, professional, wellness-warm, trust-forward, personal-brand, soft, approachable
- **Notable elements:**
  - Hero image slider with 3 full-width lifestyle/clinical photos
  - Membership plan as the primary CTA — conversion architecture built around a subscription offer
  - Icon grid layout for membership benefits (8 icons with short labels)
  - Two-column "About Dr. Reed" section with personal photo and narrative copy — humanizes the provider
  - Animated keyword reveal section: "Uncover / root cause / personalized / treatment / empowering / results"
  - Full-width footer CTA with background image and newsletter opt-in
  - Social media bar in both header and footer (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, YouTube, TikTok)
  - Supplement/skincare shop linking to third-party storefronts
  - WordPress-based
- **Best for:** Doctors, med spas, wellness coaches, functional medicine practices, any health provider building a personal brand alongside a clinical practice

## Somvai

- **URL:** <https://somvai.third-dimension.studio/>
- **What it is:** Smart nap device by Oxford/Cambridge team using radar-based technology to detect sleep cycles and wake you at the optimal moment. Pre-order on Kickstarter. Designed by Third Dimension Studio.
- **Style tags:** warm-neutral, product-forward, soft-luxury, minimalist, lifestyle-photography, nature-material, clean
- **Notable elements:**
  - Full-bleed hero: lifestyle photo of person sleeping with the product on the nightstand — product in context, not isolated on white
  - Warm greige/taupe color palette throughout — conveys calm and rest without clinical cold
  - Product has a beautiful physical design: circular LED display on a natural wood base — the hardware IS the design story
  - Pill-shaped CTA button (rounded rectangle) — softer than standard sharp-corner buttons
  - Data-backed copy cards: clickable stat panels (fatigue costs, nap science) with light hover interactions
  - Feature breakdown with labeled subcategories (Our Research / Deep Understanding / Health Improvement)
  - Minimal nav: two links + one CTA ("Pre-order on Kickstarter")
  - Team section with Oxford/Cambridge credibility callout
  - Built by Third Dimension Studio
- **Best for:** Consumer wellness products, health tech hardware, any brand where the physical product design is premium and needs to be the hero
