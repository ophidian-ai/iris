## Cascioni

- **URL:** <https://www.cascioni.com/en/home/>
- **What it is:** Mediterranean eco-retreat on Sardinia, Italy. Boutique rooms/suites, private villa, farm-to-table restaurant (Ulia Ristorante Naturale), wellness spa, and curated nature experiences.
- **Style tags:** organic, earthy, muted-palette, slow-luxury, rustic-refined, Mediterranean, editorial-photography
- **Notable elements:**
  - Watercolor/acquerello texture overlays throughout — not just flat color
  - Large editorial photography as primary visual language
  - Conde Nast Johansens award badge (social proof for luxury tier)
  - Immersive narrative copy structure ("soul experiences," "elegance of simplicity")
  - Bilingual Italian/English layout
  - Section structure tells a journey: arrival → food → wellness → nature experiences
- **Best for:** Hotels, spas, retreats, eco-lodges, any venue with strong food/nature/wellness identity
