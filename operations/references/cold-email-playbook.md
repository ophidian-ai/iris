# Cold Email Playbook

> Extracted from 5 cold email and lead generation transcripts. Updated March 2026.
> **Purpose:** Actionable reference for OphidianAI's cold outreach campaigns.

---

## Metrics & Benchmarks

- Industry average reply rate: 1-5%
- Microtargeted campaigns (under 50 recipients): 5.8% reply rate
- Broad campaigns (1,000+ recipients): 2.1% reply rate
- "Creative Ideas" campaign: 85 contacts per opportunity (best performing)
- Standard campaigns: 175-296 contacts per opportunity
- Follow-up effectiveness: 1st follow-up +49% reply boost, 3rd follow-up -20%, 4th follow-up -55%
- Inbox placement for bulk senders dropped 10-27% in Q1 2025 vs Q1 2024

## The Two-Touch Rule

1. **Email 1 (Opener):** Value offer + problem solve (80-100 words max)
2. **Email 2 (Follow-up):** 30-40 words max, bump the original message
3. **Optional Email 3:** Different angle OR recycle to new campaign
4. **No Email 4+:** Decreases reply rate by 55%

## Best Performing Format: Creative Ideas Campaign

The "3 ideas" format gets 85 contacts per opportunity (2x better than standard):

```
Hey [first name],

I had some ideas about how we could [specific thing] together. Here's what I'm thinking.

1. [Specific problem + solution tied to their business]
2. [Alternative angle they might care about]
3. [Third creative angle with different use case]

Let me know if any of these resonate.

[Signature]

P.S. If [alternative] isn't the focus right now, maybe we could [totally different idea].
```

## CTA Patterns

**Bad CTAs (10% conversion):**
- "Can we hop on a call?"
- "Do you have time tomorrow at 2 PM?"
- Why: No earned trust, asking for most valuable asset (time) upfront

**Good CTAs (30-50% qualified replies):**
- Offer something free, tangible, and specific that requires a call to deliver
- Web design: "High-converting landing page mockup for your top service"
- SEO: "I optimized your Google profile. Here's what I found in 15 minutes..."
- AI integration: "3 ways AI could automate your [specific workflow]"

## Subject Lines & Openers

**Avoid:**
- "I hope this email finds you well"
- Generic personalization (first name, job title only)

**Use:**
- Reference specific company news, funding, or hiring
- Lead with value, not a request for time

## Email Infrastructure Requirements

### Domain Setup
- 1 domain = 5 email accounts max
- 30 emails/account/day max = 150 emails/day per domain
- 5 domains = 25 accounts = 750 emails/day max
- Never use primary business domain for cold email

### Warmup
- Minimum 2 weeks before sending campaigns
- Must reach 100% health score
- SPF, DKIM, DMARC configuration required

### Monitoring
- Health score must be 100%
- Spam complaint rate: below 0.1%
- Bounce rate: below 2%

## Microtargeting Strategy

- Build campaigns around 25-50 people per micro-niche, not 1,000+
- Use narrow filters: job title, location, employee count, revenue, industry news, funding
- Create 1 campaign that feels personalized for everyone on the list
- Enrich profiles with: LinkedIn headline, summary, company news, website data

### Targeting Signals
- Recently hired team members
- Active job postings
- Recent funding
- Company news
- Specific industry pain points
- Competitor platforms in use

## Anti-Patterns

- Don't send 7-touch sequences (2023 strategy, now hurts deliverability)
- Don't use same email to 10,000 people with light personalization
- Don't ask for calls without solving a problem first
- Don't exceed 30 emails/account/day
- Don't skip deliverability setup to "get started faster"
- Don't rush to volume before fixing infrastructure
- Don't copy entire systems at once -- start with ONE friction point

## Weekly Lead Generation Loop

1. **Define** -- Pull 100-1,000 leads matching specific criteria
2. **Clean & tag** -- Remove invalid emails, segment by relevance
3. **Create copy** -- Write personalized emails (AI-generated)
4. **Send & report** -- Monitor delivery, warmup status, replies
5. **Analyze & pivot** -- See what landed, rewrite failing angles, recycle non-responders
