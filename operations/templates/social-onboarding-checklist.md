## Intake Questionnaire

Collect the following from the client:

1. What does your business do? (1-2 sentences)
2. Who is your ideal customer?
3. What 3 services/products should we highlight most?
4. What tone fits your brand? (Professional / Friendly / Bold / Casual)
5. Any topics or language to avoid?
6. Are there promotions, events, or seasonal peaks we should plan around?
7. Do you want to review every post before it goes live, or trust us to post?
8. How should customers contact you? (Phone / Website / Walk-in / Book online)
9. What days and times do you prefer posts go live?
10. Are there any days or times we should never post?

- [ ] Questionnaire received
