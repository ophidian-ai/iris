## Assessment Criteria

- **Chatbot fit:** After-hours demand, FAQ-heavy, booking/scheduling needs
- **Content fit:** Active social but inconsistent, needs regular posting
- **Review fit:** Has reviews but stale/declining, needs fresh review generation
- **Email fit:** Repeat customers, could benefit from automated follow-ups
- **Recommended tier:** Based on business size and needs
