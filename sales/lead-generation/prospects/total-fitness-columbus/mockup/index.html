Columbus' Gym Since 1998

Total Fitness of Columbus isn't a franchise, and it isn't a fad. For more than 26 years, we've been the gym where Columbus comes to train -- locals helping locals get stronger, healthier, and more confident.

We believe fitness should be accessible. That means fair pricing, no long-term contracts, and a welcoming environment for every experience level -- from first-timers to competitive athletes.

  • Locally owned and operated for 26+ years
  • No long-term contracts or hidden fees
  • Certified personal trainers on staff
  • Clean, well-maintained facility
  • Welcoming to all fitness levels
  • Convenient Columbus location
