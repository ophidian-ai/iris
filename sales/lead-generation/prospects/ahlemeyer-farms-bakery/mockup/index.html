Our Story

A Columbus Tradition Since 1989

Ahlemeyer Farms Bakery has been a cornerstone of Columbus, Indiana for over 35 years. What started as a family farm bakery has grown into one of the most beloved local institutions in Bartholomew County.

Our recipes have stood the test of time because we never cut corners. We use real butter, real eggs, and quality flour. Everything is mixed, shaped, and baked right here in our shop on 17th Street -- the same way we have always done it.

35+ Years of Trust

Generations of Columbus families have grown up with our baked goods.

Made with Real Ingredients

No shortcuts, no preservatives. Just honest, scratch-made baking.

Family Owned & Operated

A family business serving Columbus families -- that is the Ahlemeyer way.
