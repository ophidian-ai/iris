Why We Recommend Professional

Either option is a massive upgrade over your current site. But for a church with as much going on as Point of Hope, the Professional approach gives you meaningful advantages:

  • Google Business Profile -- When someone searches "church near me" or "apostolic church Indianapolis," your Google listing is what shows up first. We set up and optimize your profile so people find you.
  • Dedicated ministry pages -- Punto de Esperanza, youth ministry, Sunday School, and outreach each get their own space to tell their story instead of sharing a single scroll section.
  • Analytics -- You'll be able to see how many people visit your site, where they come from, and what they're looking for. This helps you understand your community's online behavior.
  • Unlimited revisions -- Churches often involve multiple stakeholders. With unlimited revisions, you can gather input from leadership without worrying about a revision cap.
  • AI-assisted copywriting -- We write all your page content for you using AI tools, so you don't have to worry about what to say on each page. You review and approve, we handle the writing.

At $2,500 after discounts, the Professional option is $1,300 more than Starter but includes tools and setup that would cost significantly more if added later.
