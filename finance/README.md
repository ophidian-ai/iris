# Finance Department

Financial operations, accounting, and tax management for OphidianAI.

## Structure

- `financial-tracker.md` -- Burn rate, recurring/one-time expenses, monthly summary
- `expenses/` -- Expense receipts, logs, and supporting documentation
- `invoicing/` -- Client invoices, payment records, revenue tracking
- `tax/` -- Tax preparation, estimated payments, deduction records, year-end reports
- `reports/` -- Financial reports (P&L, monthly reviews, cash flow)

## Agents

- **CFO** -- `.claude/agents/finance/cfo/` -- Strategic oversight, monthly reviews, pricing analysis
- **Financial Ops** -- `.claude/agents/finance/financial-ops/` -- Invoicing, payments, revenue logging
- **Accounting & Tax** -- `.claude/agents/finance/accounting/` -- Expenses, IRS categorization, estimated taxes
