# SOP: Google Business Profile Setup & Optimization

**Service tier:** Professional
**Owner:** OphidianAI
**Last updated:** 2026-03-05

---

## 1. Create or Claim the Listing

- Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
- Search for the client's business name
- **If listing exists:** Claim it. Verification options include postcard, phone, or email.
- **If no listing:** Create a new one.
- Postcard verification typically takes 5-14 days. Factor this into the project timeline.

## 2. Optimize the Profile

| Field | Guidance |
|---|---|
| **Business name** | Exact legal name. No keyword stuffing. |
| **Primary category** | Most specific match (e.g., "Massage Therapist" not "Health & Wellness"). |
| **Secondary categories** | Add 2-3 relevant ones. |
| **Description** | 750 chars max. Include city name and core services. Natural tone, not salesy. |
| **Phone number** | Main business line. |
| **Website** | Link to the new OphidianAI-built site. |
| **Hours** | Accurate, including special hours for holidays. |
| **Service area** | Set if the business travels to customers. |

## 3. Photos & Visual Content

- **Logo:** Square, 250x250 minimum.
- **Cover photo:** Landscape, shows business exterior or team.
- **Additional photos:** 5-10 images covering interior, products/services, and team.
- Update photos quarterly.

## 4. Posts & Updates

- Create an opening post announcing the new website at launch.
- Recommend the client posts 1-2x per month (events, offers, updates).
- Posts expire after 7 days. Event posts stay until the event date.

## 5. Reviews Strategy

- Ask the client to request reviews from 5-10 recent customers at launch.
- Provide a direct review link (search "Google review link generator" to create one).
- Coach the client to respond to all reviews within 48 hours.
- Never incentivize or fake reviews.

## 6. Link to Website

- Ensure the GBP website field links to the homepage.
- Add UTM parameters for tracking: `?utm_source=google&utm_medium=gbp`
- Verify NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) between the website and GBP listing.

## 7. Ongoing Maintenance

- Check GBP monthly as part of standard maintenance.
- Monitor for suggested edits -- Google sometimes changes info without notice.
- Update hours for holidays in advance.
- Add new photos when available.
