# OphidianAI Pricing Structure

> **Internal use only.** This is the source of truth for pricing, scoping, and tier recommendations. Not client-facing.

---

## Service Tiers

### Starter -- $2,200 - $2,500

**Best for:** Small businesses that need a clean, modern web presence.

**Scope:**
- Up to 5 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact, +1)
- Custom, modern design (mobile-first, responsive)
- Basic on-page SEO (meta tags, sitemap, semantic HTML, alt text)
- 2 rounds of revisions
- 1-2 week delivery
- Deployed on Vercel with SSL

**When to recommend:** Business has no site, or site is severely outdated/broken. They need a professional presence but don't need extensive content or advanced features.

---

### Professional -- $3,500 - $4,000

**Best for:** Businesses ready to invest in their online presence as a growth tool.

**Scope:**
- Up to 10 pages
- Custom design with advanced animations (scroll effects, transitions, hover states)
- Full SEO setup (keyword research, schema markup, Google Search Console, internal linking strategy)
- Google Business Profile setup and optimization
- AI-assisted copywriting included (all page content written for them)
- Unlimited revisions
- 2-3 week delivery
- Deployed on Vercel with SSL

**When to recommend:** Business has revenue and wants their site to actively generate leads. They value quality and don't want to write their own content. Usually replacing an existing but mediocre site.

---

### E-Commerce -- $4,500 - $6,000

**Best for:** Businesses selling products online.

**Scope:**
- Everything in Professional tier
- Product catalog with categories
- Shopping cart and checkout flow
- Stripe payment processing
- Order confirmation and notifications
- Product management via Stripe Dashboard or Supabase admin
- Delivery based on product count and complexity

**When to recommend:** Business sells physical or digital products and needs online ordering. Price scales with product count and complexity.

---

## Monthly Maintenance

| Tier | Monthly Fee |
|---|---|
| Starter / Professional | $100/mo |
| E-Commerce | $125-$150/mo |

**Included in all maintenance plans:**
- Hosting on Vercel (fast, secure, auto-scaling)
- SSL certificate
- Monthly security and performance checks
- Minor content updates (text, images)
- Uptime monitoring
- Monthly performance report (if GA4 set up)

**Not included:**
- New page additions (quoted separately, typically $200-$400/page)
- Major redesigns
- Third-party service fees (domain, email hosting)
- Content writing beyond minor edits

## Pricing Decision Criteria

Use this to determine which tier fits:

| Signal | Recommended Tier |
|---|---|
| No website or completely broken site | Starter |
| Has site but outdated, < 5 pages needed | Starter |
| Needs 6-10 pages, wants SEO | Professional |
| Wants content written for them | Professional |
| Sells products online | E-Commerce |
| Needs booking system (Vagaro, Calendly, etc.) | Professional + integration add-on |
| Needs custom functionality (portal, dashboard) | Custom quote |
