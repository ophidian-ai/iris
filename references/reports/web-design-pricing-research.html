Executive Summary

This report analyzes web design pricing in the Columbus, Indiana market and across the US to inform OphidianAI's service pricing strategy. Key findings:

Limited local competition. Only one agency (MLT Innovations) is headquartered in Columbus, IN. Most competitors serve the area from Indianapolis or Bloomington.

Pricing is opaque. Almost no local agency publishes pricing. Transparent pricing is a clear differentiator.

The market floor is ~$1,200. Freelancers start around $1,200-$1,500 for basic sites. Agencies with a local presence start at $3,000-$5,000.

Subscription models are emerging. Monthly website + marketing bundles ($599-$1,599/mo) represent a recurring revenue opportunity.

Strategic Takeaways

Market Opportunity

  • Columbus, IN is underserved. Only one local agency exists. Most competition comes from Indianapolis (1.5 hours away) or Bloomington. A local presence is a real advantage.
  • Transparent pricing wins. Almost nobody in this market publishes pricing. Listing clear packages on your site removes friction and builds trust with small business owners who don't want to "request a quote."
  • AI is a genuine differentiator. No local competitor is leveraging AI for faster delivery, better content, or smarter design. OphidianAI can deliver more for less and highlight the speed/quality advantage.

Pricing Position

Recommended approach: Price at the low-to-mid range of agency rates while delivering premium quality. This undercuts established agencies while positioning above bargain freelancers.

Consideration Detail
Market floor~$1,200 (Indy Web Design). Going below this signals low quality.
Sweet spot for small business$2,000-$5,000 for a 5-10 page site. Affordable but serious.
Agency premium range$5,000-$12,000. Justified by custom design, SEO, content.
Monthly recurring$50-$150/month for hosting + maintenance is standard and expected.

Recommended Next Steps

  • Define 2-3 service packages (e.g., Starter, Professional, Premium) with clear scope and pricing
  • Include hosting + maintenance as a monthly add-on for recurring revenue
  • Publish pricing on the OphidianAI website -- transparency is a competitive advantage
  • Consider a "Website in a Week" or fast-turnaround positioning leveraging AI speed
  • Offer a content/copywriting add-on (AI-assisted) as an upsell
