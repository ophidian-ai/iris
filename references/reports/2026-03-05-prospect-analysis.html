SAK Automotive Service & Tire

330 Center St, Columbus, IN 47201   |   (812) 372-8000   |   sakautomotive.com

Business Profile

  • Full-service auto repair and tire shop, south side of Columbus
  • Services: automotive repair, maintenance, tire rotation, installation, alignment
  • Positive reviews across Yelp and Google -- described as "honest" with "fair pricing"
  • Chamber of Commerce member -- established and reputable

Technical Assessment

Critical Issues

NO SSL/HTTPS DEAD PLATFORM NO BOOKING NO REVIEWS SHOWN CONTACT INFO HIDDEN NO STRUCTURED DATA NO OPEN GRAPH
  • Platform: HostGator Website Builder (Webzai), November 2018. Dead-end platform with zero upgrade path -- requires complete rebuild.
  • Security: No HTTPS. Every visitor sees "Not Secure" in their browser. Google actively penalizes this in search rankings.
  • SEO: No structured data (Schema.org), no Open Graph tags. Meta description has a typo ("vehcile"). Sitemap references HTTP URLs.
  • Functionality: No online booking, no reviews integration, no contact form on homepage. PayPal config still has placeholder email.
  • Mobile: Basic responsive techniques from 2018 era. Not optimized for modern mobile-first indexing.

Revenue Impact

  • Lost search traffic: No SSL means Google is ranking competitors higher for "auto repair Columbus IN" searches.
  • Lost appointments: No online booking means customers searching at 9pm go to the shop that lets them book instantly.
  • Lost trust: "Not Secure" browser warning makes visitors question the legitimacy of the business.
  • Invisible reviews: Positive Yelp and Google reviews are not displayed on the site, removing social proof at the conversion point.

Columbus Massage Center & Salon

3126 17th Street, Columbus, IN 47201   |   (812) 378-2880   |   columbusmassagecenter.com

Business Profile

  • Massage therapy, hair services, waxing, facials, and nail services
  • 11+ years of online presence (since 2013)
  • 252 reviews on LocalStylist -- consistent customer traffic
  • Listed on TripAdvisor with active Facebook presence
  • Owner: Dana Greathouse -- identifiable decision-maker

Technical Assessment

Critical Issues

BROKEN BOOKING BROKEN GIFT CARDS OUTDATED WORDPRESS THEME LEGACY ANALYTICS UNOPTIMIZED IMAGES CLUTTERED NAVIGATION
  • Platform: WordPress with Graphene theme (last updated Dec 2021). Heavy plugin dependencies: Jetpack, Contact Form 7, Vagaro, Slick Carousel.
  • Broken functionality: Vagaro booking widgets display as broken placeholders. Gift card section shows placeholder text only. Testimonials reference deprecated Google Plus API.
  • Performance: Serves unoptimized 2048px images. 15+ JavaScript dependencies. Extensive inline CSS.
  • Analytics: Still running legacy Google Analytics (not GA4) -- will lose data tracking.
  • Navigation: Cluttered menu structure with inconsistent hierarchy and too many top-level items.

Revenue Impact

  • Lost bookings: The #1 conversion action on a massage center website is broken. Every visitor who tries to book online and fails either calls (best case) or goes to a competitor (worst case).
  • Lost gift card revenue: Gift cards are pure profit drivers, especially around holidays. A broken gift card page is literally leaving money on the table.
  • Wasted investment: This business already paid for booking integration, gift cards, and intake forms. None of them work properly. They're paying for features they can't use.
  • Eroding trust: Broken elements make an otherwise established business look unprofessional or abandoned.
