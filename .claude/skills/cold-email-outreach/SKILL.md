# Cold Email Outreach

Draft cold outreach emails to potential clients for OphidianAI.

## When to Use

When Eric wants to reach out to a potential client -- typically a small business that could benefit from a new or updated website, AI tools, or AI integrations.

## Inputs

Provide as much as you have. Not all fields are required.

- **Business name**
- **Contact name** (if known)
- **Their current website** (if they have one)
- **What they need** (e.g., website redesign, new website, AI integration)
- **How Eric found them** (e.g., local search, referral, research)
- **Any specific pain points or opportunities noticed**

## Output Format

Generate a short, professional cold email with:

1. **Subject line** -- Clear, not salesy. Reference their business by name if possible.
2. **Opening** -- One sentence connecting to their business. No generic "I hope this finds you well."
3. **Value prop** -- 1-2 sentences on what OphidianAI can do for them specifically. Be concrete.
4. **Credibility** -- Brief mention of relevant work (e.g., Bloomin' Acres project if applicable).
5. **CTA** -- Simple ask. Suggest a quick call or offer to send examples.
6. **Sign-off** -- Eric Lefler, OphidianAI.

## Rules

- Keep it under 150 words. Shorter is better.
- No fluff, no filler, no buzzwords.
- No emojis.
- Sound like a real person, not a template.
- Tailor every email to the specific business. Never send generic copy.
- If Eric provides their current website, reference specific things that could be improved.
- Tone: Professional, direct, helpful -- not pushy.

## Example

**Subject:** Your website could be working harder for you

Hi [Name],

I came across [Business Name] while looking at local businesses in [area]. Your [products/services] look great, but your website isn't doing them justice -- [specific issue: slow load times, not mobile-friendly, outdated design, etc.].

I run OphidianAI, a small agency that builds modern websites for businesses like yours. I recently built a site for a local sourdough and fresh produce business and would be happy to share it as an example.

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to see if I can help?

Eric Lefler
OphidianAI
