**Pillar:** Website Tips
**Title:** 5 Seconds Is All You Get
**CTA:** Save this for later
**Image:** Pexels -- person scrolling phone looking frustrated

### Facebook (text post)

A visitor lands on your website. You have about 5 seconds before they decide to stay or leave.

Here is what makes them leave:

- The page takes forever to load
- They can not tell what you do within 3 seconds
- It looks broken on their phone
- There is no way to call, book, or message you

Here is the thing. Most of these are fixable in an afternoon.

The businesses that show up first on Google are not always the best at what they do. They just made it easy for customers to find them and take the next step.

If your website has not been updated in a while, it might be working against you without you even knowing it.
