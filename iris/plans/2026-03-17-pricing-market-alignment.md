# Implementation Plan: Pricing-Market Alignment

**Threat Level:** MEDIUM
**Date:** 2026-03-17
**Target:** Create a lower-friction entry offering that converts skeptical small-town businesses

---

## Problem Statement

Standard quote of $3,500 may be too high for the target market: small-town Indiana businesses (pop. 20K-50K) that have operated for decades without websites. No portfolio, no case studies, no social proof to justify premium pricing. Competitors include Wix/Squarespace ($16-$49/mo DIY) and local freelancers ($500-$1,500).

---

## Phase 1: Define "Quick Launch" Package (Day 1)

### Package Specs

| Attribute | Quick Launch | Current Starter |
|---|---|---|
| Price | $1,500-$1,800 | $2,200-$2,500 |
| Pages | 3 (Home, Services, Contact) | Up to 5 |
| Delivery | 5 business days | 1-2 weeks |
| SEO | Basic (title tags, meta, GBP link) | Full basic SEO |
| Revisions | 1 round | 2 rounds |
| Design | Clean template-based | Custom design |
| Maintenance | $75/mo | $100/mo |

### Positioning
- "Get your business online in a week for under $2,000"
- Not a discount -- it's a different product for a different need
- Target: businesses with ZERO web presence (FB-only, no site at all)
- Upsell path: Quick Launch -> add pages ($200-400/ea) -> full redesign later

### When to Offer
- Prospect has no website at all (just Facebook or nothing)
- Prospect pushes back on $3,500 ("we've never needed a website")
- Prospect is interested but hesitant ("let me think about it")
- First 2-3 deals (portfolio building justifies lower margin)

### When NOT to Offer
- Prospect has an existing (broken) website that needs full redesign
- Prospect explicitly needs e-commerce or complex functionality
- Prospect is already warm on the $3,500 quote

## Phase 3: Build Quick Launch Template (Days 3-5)

### Create Reusable 3-Page Template
- Home: Hero + services overview + CTA + Google Maps embed
- Services: Service list with descriptions + pricing (if applicable)
- Contact: Contact form + phone + address + hours + Google Maps
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Tailwind CSS, clean and professional
- 2-3 color scheme variants (auto, restaurant, salon)

### Streamline Delivery
- Template should be deployable in 2-3 hours of actual work
- Pre-built components: nav, footer, hero, contact form, service cards, map embed
- At $1,800 with 3 hours of work: $600/hr effective rate (excellent margin)

---

## Phase 4: First Deal Incentive (Optional)

### If Needed to Close First Deal
- Offer first Quick Launch at $1,200 (at-cost portfolio pricing)
- Frame as: "I'm building my portfolio of local businesses. I'll do your site at cost if you let me feature it."
- Only for the FIRST deal. Return to $1,800 after.
- This is a last resort -- try $1,800 first.
