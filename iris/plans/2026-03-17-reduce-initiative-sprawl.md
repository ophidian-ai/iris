# Implementation Plan: Reduce Initiative Sprawl

**Threat Level:** HIGH
**Date:** 2026-03-17
**Target:** Reduce from 8+ concurrent initiatives to 4 focused workstreams

---

## Problem Statement

8+ concurrent initiatives for a solo pre-revenue founder. No single initiative has been fully completed and delivered. Energy is distributed across cold outreach, Bloomin' Acres, website updates, Meta App, AI Products (8 products), social engine, VPS, and autonomous Iris. The result: everything is in progress, nothing is done.

---

## The Cut List

### KEEP (Revenue-Critical)

| Initiative | Why | Weekly Time Budget |
|---|---|---|
| Cold outreach + follow-ups | Only active revenue channel | 5-6 hrs/week |
| Bloomin' Acres delivery | Only active project, only path to portfolio | 8-10 hrs/week |
| Point of Hope Church close | Warmest lead, personal referral | 1-2 hrs total |
| Daily operations (morning coffee, inbox, pipeline) | Operational awareness | 3-4 hrs/week |

**Total: ~20 hrs/week on revenue-generating activities**

### FREEZE (Defer Until First Close)

| Initiative | Current State | Resume Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| AI Products template building | Pages live, no infrastructure | First recurring revenue client signed |
| VPS / Hetzner provisioning | Approved, not started | Autonomous workflows needed (post-revenue) |
| Autonomous Iris setup | Dependent on VPS | VPS provisioned |
| Social content engine enhancement | Engine built, working | Content needed for marketing push |
| Meta Developer App | Blocked on verification | Verification approved (external) |
| New skill development | 30+ skills built | Specific gap identified in delivery |
| OphidianAI website enhancements (beyond AI product pages) | Site is functional | Portfolio pieces to showcase |

### DO NOT START

- GoHighLevel white-label setup (no clients to serve)
- New prospect research beyond current pipeline (31 is enough)
- Additional outreach city expansion (5 cities is sufficient)
- Any new tooling subscriptions

## Unfreezing Rules

An initiative can only be unfrozen when:
1. First client deal is closed AND
2. Current active workstreams have capacity AND
3. The unfrozen initiative directly supports the next revenue milestone

Exception: Meta Developer App unfreezes automatically when verification is approved (no action from Eric needed, just respond to the approval).
