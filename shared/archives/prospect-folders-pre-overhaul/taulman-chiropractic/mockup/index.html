About Dr. Taulman

Two Decades of Trusted Care in Columbus

Dr. L. Scott Taulman, D.C., C.C.W.P., has been providing expert chiropractic care to Columbus, Indiana and the surrounding communities for over 20 years. His commitment to patient-centered care and evidence-based treatment has earned the trust of thousands of families.

As a Certified Chiropractic Wellness Practitioner (C.C.W.P.), Dr. Taulman goes beyond symptom relief to help patients achieve lasting wellness. His approach combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern therapeutic methods for comprehensive care.
