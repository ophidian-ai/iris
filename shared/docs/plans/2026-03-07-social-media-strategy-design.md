# Social Media Strategy Design

**Date:** 2026-03-07
**Status:** Approved

## Goal

Build a social media presence on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok that supports cold outreach credibility and generates warm inbound leads over time. AI handles content generation; Eric provides occasional on-camera anchor content.

## Key Decisions

| Decision | Choice | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Primary audience | Local business owners (potential clients) | YouTube covers AI/tech community. Social = client acquisition. |
| Content approach | Hybrid (template + anchor) | AI-generated template posts as baseline, on-camera pieces for engagement |
| Budget | Free/near-free | Solo founder, no funding. Claude + Canva Free + CapCut Free + native schedulers |
| Time commitment | 1-2 hours/week | One batch session per week |
| 90-day success metric | Credibility/warm outreach support | Prospects check socials after cold email and see active, professional presence |

---

## Platform Strategy

### Facebook
- **Primary use:** Credibility hub and local community engagement
- **Content:** Before/after website reveals, local business tips, client wins, shared articles
- **Why:** Local business owners are most active here
- **Scheduling:** Meta Business Suite (free)

### Instagram
- **Primary use:** Visual portfolio and brand presence
- **Content:** Before/after carousels, short Reels, design tips, story polls
- **Why:** Visual proof of work is compelling. Reels get algorithmic reach even with small followings
- **Scheduling:** Meta Business Suite (free)

### TikTok
- **Primary use:** Reach and discovery
- **Content:** Repurposed Reels (same short-form video), quick tips, website reaction-style content
- **Why:** Highest organic reach potential. Worth posting since content is already created for Reels
- **AI video:** CapCut and Canva for animating static before/afters into short videos
- **Scheduling:** TikTok built-in scheduler (free)

### Posting Cadence
4-5 posts/week total across platforms. Cross-post where appropriate.

## Content Framework

### Anchor Content (Eric creates, 1-2x per month)
- 60-90 second on-camera videos: quick tips, website teardowns, behind-the-scenes
- Recorded in batch (2-3 in one sitting, released over time)
- AI repurposes each into: Instagram Reel, TikTok, Facebook post with key takeaway, carousel with screenshots

### Template Content (AI generates, 3-4x per week)
- **Tip posts:** "3 things your website is doing that cost you customers"
- **Stat/insight posts:** "78% of people judge a business by its website before calling"
- **Before/after posts:** Side-by-side mockups or screenshots
- **Local callouts:** "Columbus business owners: when's the last time you Googled your own business?"
- **Industry-specific:** Rotate through niches (auto, fitness, restaurants, etc.)

### Content Pillars (rotating themes)

| Pillar | Example Topics | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Proof of work | Before/afters, client wins, portfolio pieces | 1-2x/week |
| Education | Tips, common mistakes, "did you know" | 1-2x/week |
| Local relevance | Columbus-specific, industry-specific | 1x/week |
| Behind the scenes | Building process, tools, day-in-the-life | 1x/week (when anchor content available) |

## AI Pipeline and Tooling

### Content Generation
- **Claude** (already have): Weekly batch prompt generates post copy, hashtags, captions, carousel text, video scripts
- **Skill:** `.claude/skills/social-content/` -- generates a week's batch in one prompt

### Design and Video
- **Canva Free:** Post graphics, carousels, before/after templates. Create 3-4 branded templates once, reuse weekly
- **CapCut Free:** Edit on-camera clips, add captions/text overlays, resize for Reels/TikTok
- **AI video (no-camera weeks):** CapCut and Canva animate static images into short videos

### Scheduling
- **Meta Business Suite (free):** Schedule FB and IG posts, view basic analytics
- **TikTok Scheduler (free, built-in):** Schedule TikTok posts

### Weekly Workflow (1-2 hours total)

| Step | Time | Who |
|---|---|---|
| Claude generates week's content batch | 5 min | AI |
| Review and approve/edit posts | 15-20 min | Eric |
| Create visuals in Canva (from templates) | 20-30 min | Eric (templated) |
| Schedule in Meta Business Suite + TikTok | 10 min | Eric |
| Record on-camera clip (1-2x/month) | 15-20 min | Eric |
| Edit clip in CapCut | 15-20 min | Eric (or AI-assisted) |

## Cold Outreach Integration

- Every cold email prospect will likely check socials -- active presence validates credibility
- Include social links in email signature (not in cold email body)
- After a prospect replies, follow them on Instagram/Facebook to stay in their feed

### Profile Optimization (one-time setup)
- Consistent branding across all three platforms (logo, cover image, bio)
- Bio formula: "We help [city] businesses get more customers online. Websites, SEO, and AI tools."
- Link in bio: OphidianAI.com (once built) or simple landing page
- Contact button enabled on all platforms

## 90-Day Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Posting consistency | 4-5 posts/week for 12 weeks |
| Follower growth | Not a priority (vanity metric) |
| Profile visits after cold emails | Qualitative -- do prospects check pages? |
| Content library | 50+ posts across platforms |
| Prospect engagement | Any likes, follows, or DMs from outreach targets |

### After 90 Days: Evaluate
- Is the content pipeline sustainable at 1-2 hrs/week?
- Are prospects engaging after receiving cold emails?
- Which platform drives the most profile visits?
- Consider: paid boosting on Facebook ($5-10/post) for high-performing local content
